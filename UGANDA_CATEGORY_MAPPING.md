## Executive Summary

Based on deep analysis of Uganda's two largest e-commerce platforms (Jiji.ug with 109,363 Electronics listings and Jumia.ug), your current US-style category structure needs significant revision to match Ugandan buyer search behavior.

### Key Findings:

1. **"Phones & Tablets Accessories" is MASSIVE** - 14,559 ads on Jiji.ug alone
2. **"Audio & Music Equipment" dominates** - 19,878 ads (largest Electronics subcategory)
3. **"Laptops & Computers" is separate** - Not lumped with "peripherals"
4. **Motorcycle culture is strong** - "Boda boda" is local term, 5,155+ motorcycle ads
5. **Security is major concern** - Dedicated category with 4,549 ads

## Terminology Guide: US → Uganda

### Condition Terms:
| US Term | Uganda Term |
|---------|-------------|
| Pre-owned | **Used** |
| Refurbished | **UK** or **Foreign Used** |
| Like new | **Used - Excellent condition** |
| Mint condition | **Clean** or **Perfect condition** |

### Product Terms:
| US Term | Uganda Term |
|---------|-------------|
| Computer | **Laptop** or **Desktop** (be specific) |
| Display | **Monitor** or **Screen** |
| Mobile accessories | **Phone Accessories** |
| Motorcycle gear | **Motorcycle Accessories** or **Boda Boda Gear** |
| Hi-Fi | **Audio Equipment** or **Sound System** |
| Audiophile | **Professional Audio** or **Premium Sound** |
| Gimbal | **Stabilizer** or **Gimbal Stabilizer** |
| Safe | **Safe** or **Security Box** |

## Description Writing Tips

### ✓ DO:
- State condition clearly: "Used - Excellent", "Brand New", "Foreign Used"
- Include exact specs: "8GB RAM, 128GB SSD"
- Mention "everything works perfectly" or "all original parts"
- Use "clean" to describe good condition
- Specify color/size early
- Note if item includes accessories
- Mention warranty if applicable

### ✗ DON'T:
- Use overly technical jargon without explanation
- Assume buyers know niche brand names
- Hide condition issues
- Use "refurbished" - say "UK" or "Foreign Used"
- Forget power compatibility (Uganda uses 240V, UK-style plugs)
